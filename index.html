Abhash Shrestha

Abhash Shrestha holds an MSc in Computer Science with a specialization in Machine Learning and Data Science. His master's dissertation focused on the optimization of time series algorithms, where he produced publication-worthy results by implementing novel methods to enhance both the speed and accuracy of the algorithms. These algorithms are featured in the TSML-Eval and AEON toolkits. His work demonstrated technical prowess and contributed to advancements in the field, reflecting his deep expertise in algorithm development and AI.

As a software engineer, he has played a key role in deploying large-scale, high-impact software solutions, optimizing system performance, and designing robust APIs.

Currently affiliated with CAIR, Nepal, his research explores ethical AI practices, particularly in knowledge graphs and fairness in AI. Moving forward, Abhash is driven by a vision to lead cutting-edge AI research teams, shaping the future of ethical and sustainable AI technology in real-world applications..
